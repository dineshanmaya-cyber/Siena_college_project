About Us

Siena College of Professional Studies, Edakochi, established in 2002, is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. The college is committed to providing quality education through experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and career-oriented programmes. We strive to empower students with academic excellence, professional skills, and ethical values to help them succeed in their careers and contribute positively to society.

Our Courses

Siena College offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes designed to provide students with academic excellence, practical knowledge, and career opportunities in various professional fields.

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications focuses on programming, software development, databases, networking, and web technologies.

B.Com

Bachelor of Commerce develops knowledge in accounting, finance, taxation, business management, and entrepreneurship.

BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration provides students with leadership, management, marketing, and organizational skills.

BA English

Develop communication, literature, writing, and critical thinking skills for careers in education, media, and publishing.

M.Com

Master of Commerce offers advanced knowledge in accounting, finance, banking, economics, and business research.

MCA

Master of Computer Applications focuses on software engineering, cloud computing, AI, cybersecurity, and full-stack development.
